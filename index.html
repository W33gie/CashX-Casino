The burn mechanic
Every game burns 20% of the pot in CASHX forever. Whether you win or lose, a portion is permanently removed from supply. In Dice, losing bets are burned 100%. The more games played, the rarer CASHX becomes.
FAQ

v
Dice and Hi-Lo are instant. Lottery fills when 5 players join then resolves automatically. Jackpot draws hourly.
Is the result provably fair?
v
Yes. The lottery uses a commit-reveal system on-chain. All game logic runs on public smart contracts on PulseChain.
What if nobody joins my lottery game?
v
If your game does not fill within the expiry window, your CASHX is automatically refunded to your balance.
Where do I get CASHX?
v
CASHX is available on PulseX. Swap PLS for CASHX on PulseChain mainnet. Or just deposit PLS and we swap it for you automatically.
What is the house edge?
v
No traditional house edge. The 20% burn goes permanently out of circulation. In Lottery, 80% of the pot is paid out to players.
