The burn mechanic
Every game burns 20% of the pot in CASHX forever. Whether you win or lose, a portion is permanently removed from supply. In Dice, losing bets are burned 100%. The more games played, the rarer CASHX becomes.
FAQ
How long does a game take?
v
Dice games are instant - result in under 30 seconds. Lottery games depend on how fast all 5 slots fill. Once full, the game resolves automatically within minutes.
Is the result provably fair?
v
Yes. The lottery uses a commit-reveal system where each player commits a secret before the game starts. All secrets are combined on-chain to generate the random result. Nobody can predict or manipulate it.
What if nobody joins my game?
v
If your game does not fill within the expiry window, your PLS is automatically refunded to the wallet you sent from. No action needed.
Where do I get CASHX?
v
CASHX is available on PulseX. Swap PLS for CASHX directly in the PulseX app or any PulseChain DEX. Make sure you are on PulseChain mainnet.
What is the house edge?
v
There is no traditional house edge. The 20% burn goes permanently out of circulation, not to a house wallet. In the Lottery, 80% of the pot is paid out. In Dice, payouts are calibrated so the burn sustains the house over time.
